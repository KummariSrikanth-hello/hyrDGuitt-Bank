About Our Credit Cards

Our credit cards offer you the freedom to spend smartly, earn rewards, and manage your finances conveniently. Choose from a range of cards that suit your lifestyle — from shopping and travel to premium and business needs.

Features of Our Credit Cards

  • Wide range of cards: Classic, Gold, Platinum, and Premium
  • Attractive reward points on every spend
  • Cashback offers and discounts at partner stores
  • EMI conversion on big purchases
  • Contactless payment feature
  • Secure online and international transactions
  • Fuel surcharge waiver and airport lounge access (on select cards)

Advantages of Our Credit Cards

  • Up to 50 days interest-free credit period
  • Easy bill payments and utility payments
  • Track expenses with online statements
  • Lost card liability cover
  • Special privileges for premium customers

Documents Required

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card / Passport / Voter ID
  • Latest salary slip / ITR / bank statement (for income proof)
  • Address proof
  • Passport size photograph

Terms & Conditions (Highlights)

  • Annual fee applicable as per card type
  • Late payment charges apply if dues are not cleared on time
  • Credit limit decided based on eligibility and bank policy
