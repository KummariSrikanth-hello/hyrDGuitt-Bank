About Our Current Account

Our Current Account is designed for businesses, entrepreneurs, and professionals who require frequent transactions and easy access to their funds. Enjoy high transaction limits, overdraft facilities, and value-added services that help you manage your business finances efficiently.

Features of Our Current Account

  • Unlimited transactions with no restrictions
  • Overdraft facility (subject to approval)
  • Free cheque book and demand draft facilities
  • Internet and mobile banking with bulk payment options
  • Multi-city cheques and cash handling at any branch
  • Free monthly statements
  • SMS and email alerts for transactions

Advantages of Our Current Account

  • Easy cash deposit and withdrawal across branches
  • Helps maintain business cash flow effectively
  • Customized solutions for SMEs, corporates, and professionals
  • Free advisory services for your financial needs
  • Priority service at branches
  • Free digital banking tools to manage accounts online

Documents Required

  • PAN Card (Proprietor/Company/Partnership)
  • Business registration certificate / GST certificate
  • Address proof (utility bill, lease agreement, etc.)
  • Identity proof of authorized signatories
  • Passport size photographs

Terms & Conditions (Highlights)

  • Overdraft limit subject to approval and review
  • Charges applicable for non-maintenance of minimum balance
