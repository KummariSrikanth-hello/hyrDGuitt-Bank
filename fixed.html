About Our Fixed Deposit

Our Fixed Deposit (FD) offers a secure and guaranteed return on your savings. Choose flexible tenures, earn attractive interest rates, and enjoy peace of mind with safe investments. Whether for short-term or long-term goals, our FD helps you grow your wealth steadily.

Features of Our Fixed Deposit

  • Attractive and assured interest rates
  • Flexible tenure from 7 days to 10 years
  • Minimum deposit starting at ₹1,000
  • Loan/overdraft facility against FD up to 90% of the deposit
  • Automatic renewal facility available
  • Interest payout options: monthly, quarterly, or at maturity

Advantages of Our Fixed Deposit

  • Guaranteed returns with zero risk
  • Higher interest rates for senior citizens
  • Helps plan for future financial goals
  • Tax-saving FD options under section 80C
  • Premature withdrawal facility (with applicable charges)

Documents Required

  • PAN Card / Form 60
  • Aadhaar Card or any valid ID proof
  • Address proof (utility bill, passport, etc.)
  • Passport size photograph

Terms & Conditions (Highlights)

  • Interest rates vary based on tenure and amount
  • Premature closure penalty may apply
  • TDS applicable as per income tax rules
