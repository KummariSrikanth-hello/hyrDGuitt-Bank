Mini Statement

A mini statement gives you a quick view of your recent account activity. It displays the last few transactions (usually 5-10), including deposits, withdrawals, and your current balance. You can access it via ATM, mobile banking, internet banking, or SMS.

Why Use a Mini Statement?

  • Quickly check your recent transactions without downloading a full statement
  • Monitor for unauthorized or unexpected activity
  • View your current account balance on the go
  • Track recent payments, deposits, or withdrawals anytime
  • Access your account summary securely, 24x7
